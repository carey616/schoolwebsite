Admissions

Join Our School

We welcome students from Pre-primary (PP1 & PP2) to Grade 8. Our admissions are open throughout the year based on availability of space.

Requirements:

  • ✔️ Copy of birth certificate
  • ✔️ 2 passport-size photos
  • ✔️ Previous school report (if applicable)

How to Apply:

Visit our school during working hours or email us at admissions@sunrise.ac.ke.
Admission forms are available at the school office.
